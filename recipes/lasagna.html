This is Lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish known for its rich layers of pasta, flavorful meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked to perfection, it offers a hearty and satisfying meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions. Each bite delivers a comforting blend of textures and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground Chicken
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 can (29 ounces) tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 envelope (1 ounce to 1 1/2 ounces) spaghetti sauce mix
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 pound lasagna noodles, uncooked
  • 12 ounces ricotta cheese or small curd cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 2 cans (12 ounces each) tomato juice
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, brown ground beef in vegetable oil.
  2. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, spaghetti mix, and sugar.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In the bottom of a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan, layer uncooked lasagna noodles, ground beef mixture, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese, starting with noodles and ending with ground beef mixture, ricotta, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Pour tomato juice over lasagna until the juice reaches the top of the pan; sprinkle top with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
  7. Cut into squares to serve.
